Tommy Bahama Outdoor

Home All Products Ttommy Bbahama Outdoor

Soft contemporary designs feature clean lines and an elegant fusion of materials that convey the sophisticated look of English walnut and woven leather. Aluminum frames feature an artisan dry-brushed finish that replicates the unique grain lines in wood. Flat woven all-weather wicker, in a refined basket weave pattern, is finished to emulate the rich look of leather. Occasional tables feature durable white sintered stone tops with subtle gray veining, offering the classic look of Carrera marble. The Abaco collection represents a designer approach to outdoor living with an assortment comprised of 28-pieces, including bistro and large-scale dining, occasional tables, deep seating, and a customizable sectional.
